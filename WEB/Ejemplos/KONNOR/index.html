Thunder Bikes was founded in 2010 with a clear goal: to create high-performance mountain bikes that blend innovation, durability, and design. The company began as a small workshop in the foothills of the Pyrenees, where a group of cycling enthusiasts started handcrafting bikes for local riders. Passion for adventure and precision engineering quickly became the brand’s identity.

Over the years, Thunder Bikes invested heavily in research and development, introducing lightweight frames made from advanced alloys and carbon fiber. Their models began to stand out in regional competitions, gaining recognition for their balance between strength and agility.

By 2015, the company expanded its facilities and started exporting to several European countries. The brand’s philosophy—“Ride beyond limits”—inspired a growing community of riders who sought quality and reliability on every trail.

Today, Thunder Bikes continues to design and manufacture all its bikes in-house, maintaining strict quality standards and a sustainable production process. With a blend of tradition and cutting-edge technology, Thunder Bikes remains committed to pushing the boundaries of mountain biking worldwide.
