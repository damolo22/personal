Welcome to Aeterna

"A Modern European Sanctuary in the Golden City."
Nestled within the historic walls of Old Town Prague, Aeterna is an exclusive dining sanctuary founded on the pursuit of the timeless supper. Our restaurant emerged from the vision of Chef Julian Martel, who sought to fuse the classic precision of European cuisine with a philosophy unburdened by fleeting trends.
Established in a meticulously restored 19th-century ballroom, Aeterna's origin is rooted in the city's rich architectural heritage. Every dish is a reflection of seasonal abundance, transforming local Czech ingredients and techniques from the Mediterranean coast into lasting culinary memories. We invite you to experience an evening where the grand tradition of Prague meets the art of modern, intimate dining.
